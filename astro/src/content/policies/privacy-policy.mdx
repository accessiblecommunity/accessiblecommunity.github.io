## Introduction

This privacy policy for Accessible Community ("us", "we", or "our") describes
how and why we might collect, store and/or share your information when you use
our services (“Services”). These services include visiting and using our web
content and services (“Sites”) at:

- accessiblecommunity.org
- ta11y.org
- mutua11y.org,
- loca11y.org

We use your Personal Information only for providing and improving Accessible
Community's sites, tools and services. By using the Sites, you agree to the
collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## How do we collect information?

We collect information that you provide to us when you register for our
services, use our sites or otherwise contact us.

The categories of information we will be collecting in the next 12 months are:

- <span class="fw-bold">Identifiers</span> such as account name, email, and IP
  address

- <span class="fw-bold">Personal Information</span> such as name and
  disability-related information

- <span class="fw-bold">Geolocation data</span> such as device location

- <span class="fw-bold">Audio and visual information</span> such as images and
  audio in connection with our business activities

### User Provided Information

While using our Sites, we may ask you to provide us with certain personal
information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally
identifiable information may include, but is not limited to your name and
email address ("Personal Information"). We may also as you to provide us with
sensitive information about your disability status (“Sensitive Information”).

### Log Data

Like many site operators, we collect information that your browser sends
whenever you visit, use, or navigate our Sites and Services ("Log Data").

This Log Data may include device and usage information such as your computer's
Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of
our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on
those pages, your location and other statistics.

This information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of
our services, for internal analytics and reporting purposes. We may collect
this information through cookies and similar technologies.

### Cookies

Cookies are files with small amounts of data, which may include an anonymous
unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored
on your computer's hard drive.

Like many sites, we use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct
your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some
portions of our Site. You can learn more about this in our Cookie Policy at [https://accessiblecommunity.org/about/policies](/about/policies).

### Social Media Data

We may provide you with the option to register with us using an existing
social media or email account. If you choose to register in this way, we may
have access to information from the social media provider such as your name,
email, friends list, profile image, etc. We do not control and are not
responsible for information with your third-party social media provider. We
will store only the information needed to provide the services.

## How do we use your information?

We use your Personal Information and Sensitive Information for providing and
improving Accessible Community's Sites and Services, such as:

- Facilitate account creating, authentication and managing user accounts.
- Deliver services to the user.
- Respond to user inquiries and offer support to the users.
- Save and protect an individual's vital interest such as to prevent harm.

<span class="fw-bold">
  We do not share your Personal Information or Sensitive Information.
</span>

### Communications

We may use your Personal Information to periodically contact you with
newsletters and other information to keep you up to date about our activities
and request your assistance. You may opt out of these communications.

### Data Retention

We only keep your Personal and Sensitive Information for as long as it is
necessary to provide Services to you. We will not keep your information longer
than the time you have an account with us.

You have a right to request the deletion of your personal data by emailing [accounts@accessiblecommunity.org](mailto:accounts@accessiblecommunity.org).

## Is your information secure?

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember
that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic
storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means
to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute
security.

### Third-Party Websites

We are not responsible for the safety of any information you share with third
parties that we may link to but who are not affiliated with our Sites or
Services.

### Data Transferred Internationally

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing our Sites
or Services outside the United States, your information may be transferred to,
stored, and used by us in the United States.

## Do we collect information from minors?

We do not knowingly solicit data from children under 18 years of age. By using
our Sites and Services, you agree that you are at least 18 years of age or you
are the parents or guardian of a minor and give consent to the minor
dependent's use of our Sites and Services.

## What legal bases do we rely on when using your information?

### For Users in the EU or UK

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR require us to
explain the legal bases we rely on to use your Personal and Sensitive
Information.

- <span class="fw-bold">Consent</span>: We may use your personal and sensitive
  information if you have given us permission to use it for a specific purpose.
  You can withdraw your consent at any time by closing your account or emailing
  [accounts@accessiblecommunity.org](mailto:accounts@accessiblecommunity.org)
  and requesting your account be closed.

- <span class="fw-bold">Legal Obligations</span>: We may use your information to
  comply with legal obligations such as cooperation with law enforcement bodies
  or regulatory agencies.

- <span class="fw-bold">Vital Interests</span>: We may use our information if we
  believe it is necessary to protect the vital interests of you or a third
  party, such as situations involving potential threats to the safety of any
  person.

### For Users in Canada

We may use your personal and sensitive information if you have given us
specific permission (express consent) to use it for a specific purpose or in
situations where your permission can be inferred (implied consent).

You can withdraw your consent at any time by closing your account or emailing [accounts@accessiblecommunity.org](mailto:accounts@accessiblecommunity.org) and requesting your account be closed.

In some exceptional cases, we may be legally permitted under applicable law to
use your information without your consent, such as if disclosure is required
to comply with a subpoena, warrant, court order, or rules of the court
relating to the production of records.
